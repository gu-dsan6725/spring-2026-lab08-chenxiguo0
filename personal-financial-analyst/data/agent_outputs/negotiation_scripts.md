# Negotiation Scripts & Strategies

Here are specialized negotiation scripts to help lower your existing recurring bills.

## Comcast Internet
**Strategy:** Mention competitive rates and emphasize customer loyalty.
**Script:**
> "Hi, I've been a loyal Comcast customer, but I recently noticed my bill has crept up to $89.99/month. T-Mobile is currently offering 5G Home Internet in my area for $50/month. I'd prefer to stay with Comcast to avoid the hassle of switching equipment. Can you apply any current promotions to my account or match this competitor rate?"

## Planet Fitness / Local Gym
**Strategy:** Ask for an annual pre-payment discount.
**Script:**
> "Hello, I currently pay $24.99/month for my membership. Since I've been coming regularly, I was wondering if you offer a discounted rate for paying for the entire year upfront?"
