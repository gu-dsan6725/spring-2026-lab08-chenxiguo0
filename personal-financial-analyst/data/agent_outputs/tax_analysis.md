# Tax Analysis & Deductions

Reviewing the transactions for potential tax implications or deductions:

### Home Office & Utilities
If you work from home as a 1099 contractor, a portion of your utilities (PG&E, Comcast Internet, Water, Gas) may be deductible. 
*Recommendation:* Calculate the square footage of your dedicated home office relative to your total home size, and apply that percentage to the utility bills.

### Health & Wellness
The gym memberships (Planet Fitness / Equinox) are generally **not** deductible as medical expenses unless specifically prescribed by a physician to treat a specific medical condition.

### Income
Ensure all paycheck deposits are reconciled with your W-2s or 1099s at the end of the year.
